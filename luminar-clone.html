Frequently Asked Questions

What courses does Luminar Technolab offer? ▼

Luminar Technolab offers a wide range of courses including Full Stack Development, Python & Django, Data Science & AI/ML, Mobile App Development, Cloud Computing, Cyber Security, UI/UX Design, and more.

Do you provide placement assistance? ▼

Yes! We provide 100% placement assistance with a dedicated placement cell. We have 500+ hiring partners including top MNCs and startups. Our placement record stands at 10,000+ successful placements.

Are the courses available online? ▼

Yes, Luminar Technolab offers both online and offline (classroom) training modes. You can choose the mode that suits your schedule and learning preferences.

What is the duration of the courses? ▼

Course durations range from 3 to 6 months depending on the program. We also offer weekend and fast-track batches for working professionals.

Yes, upon successful completion of the course, you will receive a globally recognized certificate from Luminar Technolab that is valued by top companies across India and abroad.
